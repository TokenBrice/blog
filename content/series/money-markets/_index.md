Money markets are the core lending primitive of DeFi. This series starts with risk assessment, then moves into user strategies and protocol-level innovations such as liquidations, self-repaying loans, and collateral design.

## Reading Path

1. [Assessing risk in decentralized finance](/money-markets-risk/) builds a qualitative risk framework for Aave, Compound, Cream, and similar markets.
2. [DeFi money markets cookbook](/money-market-recipes/) turns the primitive into practical strategies.
3. [Understanding innovations in money markets](/money-market-innovations/) looks at liquidations, self-repaying loans, and future lending design.
