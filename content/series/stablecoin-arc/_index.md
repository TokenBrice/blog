Stablecoins are DeFi's settlement layer, primary unit of account, and most battle-tested product. This series follows the design space from early fiat-backed models to algorithmic experiments, resilient collateralized systems, liquidity engines, and the search for a more decentralized endgame.

## Reading Path

1. [Is stability a necessity for cryptocurrencies mainstream adoption?](/stablecoins/) introduces the basic stablecoin problem: volatility, collateral, and why price stability matters.
2. [The state and future of algorithmic stablecoins](/algorithmic-stablecoins/) examines the first wave of algorithmic models and their core stabilization challenge.
3. [The tale of two seigniorage models: Basis vs ESD](/seigniorage-basis-esd/) compares two major templates for elastic stablecoin supply.
4. [Can Aave's GHO stablecoin help the protocol further grow?](/aave-gho-stablecoin/) studies a lending-protocol-native stablecoin.
5. [Chicken Bonds](/lusd-chicken-bonds/) explores liquidity bootstrapping for LUSD.
6. [The Marauder's Map of Decentralized Stablecoins](/stablecoin-marauder-map/) maps modern decentralized stablecoin mechanisms.
7. [Why Polaris?](/why-polaris/) explains the thesis behind building a counterparty-free stablecoin.
