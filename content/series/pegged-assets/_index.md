Pegged assets include stablecoins, tokenized BTC, liquid staking tokens, and other assets designed to track a reference. This short series connects the production side of pegged assets with the liquidity venues that make them usable.

## Reading Path

1. [Exploring stable assets on Ethereum](/ethereum-stable-assets/) explains why pegged assets matter, how they are produced, and where the tradeoffs appear.
2. [The Pegged Asset Swap Wars](/pegged-assets-swap/) examines the liquidity venues that serve correlated pairs, from StableSwap to debt-driven liquidity.
